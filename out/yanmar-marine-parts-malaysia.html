Frequently Asked Questions

How long does sourcing Yanmar marine parts take in Malaysia?

For common series (3JH, 4JH, 2GM) we typically confirm availability within 24 hours. Parts in stock in Malaysia ship same or next day. Sourced-from-Japan parts usually arrive in 5–10 working days. Emergency cases get priority handling.

Do you supply Yanmar OEM parts or aftermarket?

We source both. OEM parts come with full Yanmar traceability. Quality aftermarket alternatives (for wear items like impellers, filters, zincs) are clearly identified and cost 30–50% less. You choose.

Can you ship to Sabah and Sarawak?

Yes — East Malaysia is a core market for us. Fishing fleets and dive operators in Kota Kinabalu, Sandakan, Miri, and Kuching regularly rely on us when local stockists run dry. We use reliable couriers with full tracking.

My Yanmar serial number is worn off. Can you still identify the part?

Yes. Send us a photo of the engine, the part, and any visible markings. Our team can usually cross-reference using casting numbers, dimensions, or the vessel's registration records.

Do you stock Yanmar saildrive parts?

Yes — SD50, SD60, and SD20 saildrives are within scope. Bellows, seals, prop shaft seals, and leg oil seals are common requests we fulfil regularly.
